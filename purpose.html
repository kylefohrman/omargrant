Space Coast, Florida USA is often referred to as America’s second Silicon Valley. However, unlike the San Francisco Bay area, zero technology-focused investment funds call Florida’s space coast home. Or rarely even take a gander south of the Mason-Dixon line.

Recognizing that their vision of building a better tomorrow would proceed at a snail’s pace without funding, a team of the world‘s best scientists and brightest engineers set out to solve and find a solution.

Omar Grant came out of this plan. Designed to bring fun and joy to our loyal customers, some of the profits generated by Omar Grant will be directed into the research needed to crack several of the hardest problems facing our civilization. Profits for Purpose is our philosophy. Our vision is to drive solutions from innovative technologies that can fundamentally improve the quality of life for everyone, globally.
